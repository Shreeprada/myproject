APPLICATION FORM FOR THE TEACHING ASSISTANTSHIP FOR THE YEAR 2016-17

Teaching Assistantship to Non-GATE and Non-sponsored Full time M.Tech/M.Sc[Engg] students under TEQIP-II. This is to inform all the concerned that there is a provision for awarding Teaching Assistantship of Rs.8,000/- per month for the students pursuing full time M.Tech and M.Sc[Engg] programs of the college. The Non-GATE and non-sponsored students admitted to the first semester M.Tech programs for the academic year 2016-17 with PGCET ranking and those who have registered for M.Sc[Engg] by research are eligible to apply for Teaching Assistantship under Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme[TEQIP], Phase-II. The award and continuation of teaching assistantship shall be based on the norms and availability of funds under TEQIP-II. The eligible students shall submit their applications online.
